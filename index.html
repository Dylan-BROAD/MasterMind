WELCOME TO MASTERMIND

In this game, you will attempt to beat the computer and crack the color code within 10 tries!

Some tips for your game.

To start, select 4 colors out of the color table and then press submit to place your guess on the board! Your guess will will show hints for you on the left of the board.

These hints are as follows:

BLACK circles indicate correct color and correct position.

WHITE circles indicate correct color but wrong position.

GRAY circles indicate incorrect color and incorrect position.

If at any point you wish to reset the game and board with it, you can press the reset button.

If you wish to clear your color choices you can also press the clear button.
